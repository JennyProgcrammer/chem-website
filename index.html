Chemistry Infographics

These infographics from prelim to midterm from every group of Section BSIT 201 represent a significant milestone, as they embody the shared vision, creativity, and dedication of the entire collective. This website brings together the diverse contributions and accomplishments of each group into a unified and cohesive whole. The insights gained, lessons learned, and best practices established during the creation process of each infographic by the group lay the groundwork for ongoing improvement, growth, and collaboration within the section.
